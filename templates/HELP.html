WELCOME TO T-GIVE Nexus

T-Give Nexus Rules

  1. In order to access any page, you MUST login even after Registration.
  2. After placing an Order, it takes about 30-50 minutes before being processed. if your order was not valid or you didn't have consent of it, message our Customer Care to cancel the order befor the 30-50 minutes delay time elapses.
  3. All quantity input MUST be positive numbers and minimum 1.
  4. The extention of profile image MUST be any of this; [.jpg, .png, .jpeg, .gif].
  5. Changing Mobile number is not Recommended, if you wish to change it message our Customer Care for Assistance.
  6. Deleting an Account is permanent and can be undone. All the account data will be erased permanently.
  7. If you Forgot your T-Give Nexus account password message our Customer care for Assistance.

T-Give Nexus Help

  1. To view product details >> tap the product you are interested to view it's details.
  2. To rate the selected product >> click the number of stars you wish to symbolise the rate number you wish to give, and click the 'submit' button.
  3. To return back to products >> click the button named '✖'.
  4. To add product to cart >> click the button named 'add to cart'.
  5. To increase or lower the quantity of product >> click the round button named '-' to lower or '+' to increase.
  6. To view product in cart >> click the button named 'view cart' located bottom-left.
  7. To continue the ordering process >> click the button named'proceed to checkout'.
  8. Finally, to order product >> click the button named 'Confirm Order'.
  9. To add a profile picture >> navigate to Update profile — tap the change profile picture — select the picture you want — click the upload button.
  10. To change password or delete your account >> navigate to Account Settings — scroll to the part you wish to modify.
  11. To message Customer Care >> navigate to Message Us and enter the message you wish to send.
  12. Incase of forgotten password >> message us for support and assistance.

T-Give Nexus Recommendations
